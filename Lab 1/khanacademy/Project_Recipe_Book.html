Chicken Avocado Wrap

  • Time: Approximately 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 1
Avocado 1
Lettuce as needed
Tomato 1
Tortilla wraps 2-4
Greek yogurt optional

Step 1: Grill or cook chicken breast.
Step 2: Mash avocado and spread on a tortilla.
Step 3: Layer with lettuce, tomato, and grilled chicken. Add Greek yogurt if desired.

Vegetable Fried Rice

  • Time: Approximately 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked rice 2 cups
Mixed veggies (peas, carrots, corn) 1 cup
Eggs 2
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Sesame oil 1 tbsp
Green onions as needed

Step 1: Scramble eggs in a wok; add mixed veggies.
Step 2: Stir in cooked rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
Step 3: Garnish with chopped green onions.

Baked Lemon Garlic Salmon

  • Time: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Salmon fillets 4
Lemon 1
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Dill 1 tsp
Salt and pepper to taste

Step 1: Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet.
Step 2: Drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, and sprinkle garlic, dill, salt, and pepper.
Step 3: Bake until salmon is cooked through.
